### I. Spring Clothing (Cotton, Thin Denim, Knitted Cotton, Lightweight Synthetic)

**1. Pure Cotton (Spring Shirts, T-shirts, Casual Pants)**
*   **Washing:** Machine or hand wash; water temperature $\le$ 30°C; use neutral detergent. Wash light and dark colors separately. Add a pinch of salt during the first wash to set the color. For machine wash, use a laundry bag + gentle cycle to prevent pilling.
*   **Care:** Air dry in a cool, ventilated place; avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading. Ensure the garment is completely dry before storage. Fold or use wide-shoulder hangers. Use desiccants during humid weather to prevent mold.

**2. Thin Denim (Spring Jeans, Denim Jackets)**
*   **Washing:** Water temperature $\le$ 30°C; use neutral detergent. Wash inside out to reduce fading. Use the gentle cycle for machine washing. Avoid frequent washing; once every 1–2 weeks is sufficient.
*   **Care:** Air dry inside out; avoid direct sunlight. Store folded flat or hanging. Use wide-shoulder hangers to prevent waist deformation. Place moisture-proof paper inside pockets to maintain the shape.

**3. Knitted Cotton (Spring Sweaters, Thin Cardigans)**
*   **Washing:** Hand wash preferred; water temperature $\le$ 25°C. Gently press with neutral detergent. For machine wash, use a laundry bag and select the "Knitwear" mode. Do not scrub or wring forcefully.
*   **Care:** Dry flat in the shade to avoid stretching the neckline. Store folded. Use mothballs to prevent insects. Use a fabric shaver for minor pilling.

**4. Lightweight Synthetic (Spring Trench Coats, Sun-protective Clothing)**
*   **Washing:** Machine washable; water temperature 30-40°C. Use neutral or alkaline detergent. Rub gently; lightly brush stubborn stains. Add fabric softener during washing to reduce static.
*   **Care:** Suitable for sun drying; flip promptly to ensure total dryness. Store either folded or hanging. Avoid heavy pressure to prevent permanent creasing.

### II. Summer Clothing (Silk, Linen, Ice Silk, Short-sleeve Cotton, Chiffon)

**1. Silk (Summer Dresses, Blouses)**
*   **Washing:** Dry clean recommended. For hand wash, use specialized silk neutral detergent; water temperature $\le$ 25°C. Soak for $\le$ 15 minutes and press gently. Do not scrub, wring, or bleach.
*   **Care:** Dry in a cool, ventilated place; avoid sun exposure. Use smooth hangers to prevent snagging. Avoid heavy pressure when folded. Use low-temperature steam ironing at a 1-2cm distance from the fabric.

**2. Linen/Cotton-Linen (Summer T-shirts, Wide-leg Pants, Shirts)**
*   **Washing:** Machine or hand wash; water temperature $\le$ 30°C; use neutral detergent. Linen wrinkles easily, so do not scrub forcefully. Wash light and dark colors separately.
*   **Care:** Can be sun-dried; store immediately after drying. Iron flat before storage. Store in a dry environment to prevent mold.

**3. Ice Silk (Summer T-shirts, Shorts, Dresses)**
*   **Washing:** Hand or machine wash; water temperature $\le$ 30°C; use neutral detergent. Use a laundry bag and gentle cycle for machine washing. Do not soak for more than 10 minutes.
*   **Care:** Air dry in the shade; avoid sun exposure or high-heat drying. Store folded flat. Avoid contact with sharp or rough objects to prevent snagging.

**4. Chiffon (Summer Dresses, Sun-protective Shirts)**
*   **Washing:** Hand wash preferred; water temperature $\le$ 30°C. Rinse gently with neutral detergent. For machine wash, use a laundry bag and gentle cycle. Do not wring forcefully.
*   **Care:** Air dry in the shade. Use thin hangers but be careful to avoid snagging. Place a layer of tissue paper between folds to prevent sticking. Use low-temperature steam for light wrinkles.

### III. Autumn Clothing (Wool, Cashmere, Heavy Denim, Corduroy, Suede)

**1. Wool/Cashmere (Autumn Sweaters, Light Cashmere Coats)**
*   **Washing:** Dry clean preferred. For hand wash, use wool-specific detergent; water temperature $\le$ 20°C. Soak for $\le$ 15 minutes and press gently. No machine wash, scrubbing, or wringing.
*   **Care:** Dry flat in the shade; do not hang to avoid stretching. Use moth repellents (mothballs, lavender sachets) and wrap in breathable cloth bags. Use wide-shoulder hangers or fold without heavy pressure.

**2. Heavy Denim (Autumn Denim Jackets, Thick Jeans)**
*   **Washing:** Water temperature $\le$ 30°C; use neutral detergent. Wash inside out to reduce fading and wear. Use the standard machine cycle. Soak in salt water for 30 minutes during the first wash to set color.
*   **Care:** Air dry inside out; avoid sun exposure. Store folded flat or on wide-shoulder hangers. Ensure 100% dry before long-term storage and use desiccants.

**3. Corduroy (Autumn Corduroy Jackets, Pants)**
*   **Washing:** Hand or machine wash; water temperature $\le$ 30°C; neutral detergent. Use a laundry bag and gentle cycle for machine wash. Do not scrub forcefully to avoid damaging the pile/texture.
*   **Care:** Air dry in the shade, facing the reverse side up. Store folded; avoid heavy pressure which crushes the pile. Iron with low-temperature steam through a pressing cloth in the direction of the pile.

**4. Suede (Autumn Suede Coats, Jackets)**
*   **Washing:** Dry clean recommended. For synthetic suede, hand wash at $\le$ 30°C with neutral detergent and press gently. No machine wash, bleaching, or wringing.
*   **Care:** Air dry in the shade; avoid sun and high heat. Store hanging to prevent creases from folding. Use a soft-bristled brush to gently remove surface dust.

### IV. Winter Clothing (Down Jackets, Heavy Wool Coats, Fleece-lined Denim, Thermal Underwear)

**1. Down Material (Winter Down Jackets, Vests)**
*   **Washing:** Dry clean preferred. For washable types, use down-specific detergent; water temperature $\le$ 30°C. Soak for $\le$ 20 minutes and press gently. For machine wash, use down-specific mode and a laundry bag.
*   **Care:** Air dry in a cool place. Pat gently while drying to restore fluffiness. Ensure total dryness before storage. Do not over-compress in vacuum bags. Periodically take out to pat and ventilate.

**2. Heavy Wool Coat (Winter Wool/Cashmere Overcoats)**
*   **Washing:** MUST dry clean. Dry cleaning protects the elasticity and softness of wool fibers. No water wash or machine wash to avoid felting and shrinking.
*   **Care:** Store on wide-shoulder, non-marking hangers away from moisture and heat. Pat off dust before storage. Use moth repellents and keep away from sharp objects.

**3. Fleece-lined Denim (Winter Fleece Jeans, Jackets)**
*   **Washing:** Water temperature $\le$ 30°C; neutral detergent. Wash inside out; use the gentle cycle for machine wash. Avoid long soaking to prevent the fleece layer from shedding.
*   **Care:** Air dry inside out; avoid sun exposure. Store folded flat; avoid heavy pressure on the fleece. Dry immediately after washing to prevent mold or odors.

**4. Thermal Underwear (Cotton or Dralon Thermals)**
*   **Washing:** Machine or hand wash; water temperature $\le$ 30°C; neutral detergent. Use the gentle machine cycle; do not bleach.
*   **Care:** Air dry in the shade or short-term sun exposure. Store folded flat in a dry place. For Dralon materials, avoid high-heat ironing to protect the thermal fibers.

---

### V. General Care Tips

1. Wash different materials separately to avoid staining and wear. Ensure detergent is fully dissolved before adding clothes to prevent local discoloration.
2. Treat stains immediately; the longer they sit, the harder they are to remove. Use specific treatments (e.g., dish soap for grease, cold water for blood).
3. Check the care label before ironing and adjust the temperature accordingly. Always test iron on an inconspicuous inside area first.
4. For long-term storage, ensure clothes are 100% dry and check periodically for mold or insect damage.
